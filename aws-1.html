Aws Certified Solutions Architect- Associate Certification

  • What is  Aws Certified Solutions Architect- Associate Certification?

    AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate falls under the category of technical certifications offered by Amazon Web Services (AWS) for beginners and professionals.  AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate certification is one of the most demanded qualifications for cloud engineers is. This certification endorses the ability to effectively demonstrate knowledge of how to architect and deploy secure and resilient applications on AWS technologies.

  • Why AWS Certified Solutions Architect- Associate Course?

    The on-demand availability of cloud computing resources has improved enormously within the last years and it will further continue to grow abruptly. AWS is currently the driving force of the cloud computing industry with millions of global clients. A vast amount of businesses has begun utilizing it, which has resulted in generating the need for knowledgeable professionals with the proficiency to handle such tasks.  Thus this era is creating a pool of opportunity for professionals to build a secure and lucrative career. Since AWS is globally accepted, becoming certified will yield even more expansive opportunities. The Solutions Architect certification can be a launchpad for an architect role — with the right experience and success ladder.

  • Who can take AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate exam?

    The AWS solution architect training is designed for beginners and an aspirant with at least one year of experience working as a solutions architect on the AWS platform.
