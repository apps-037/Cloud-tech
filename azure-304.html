AZ-304: Microsoft Azure Architect Design Certification

    The designer (AZ-301/304) will be focusing on capturing business needs using real- world examples, constraints and business objectives and selecting solution implementations based on the organization’s request.

Responsibilities for an Azure Solution Architect include advising stakeholders and translating business requirements into secure, scalable, and reliable cloud solutions.
